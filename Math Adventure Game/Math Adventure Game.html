Addition

Addition is a basic math operation where you combine two or more numbers to get a total. It's like putting things together. For example, if you have 2 apples and get 3 more, you now have 5 apples. The plus sign (+) shows that you are adding numbers. Addition helps us count and understand amounts in daily life.Addition is one of the fundemental operation in mathetics.



Addition is the mathematical process of combining two or more numbers to get a total or sum. For example, if you add 2 and 3, the sum is 5. The symbol for addition is "+".

Here's the basic concept:

a+b=c Where:

a and b are the numbers being added.

c is the sum or result of the addition.
